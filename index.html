CLV as a Service

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV or LTV) is the most important marketing metric you are not effectively tracking. You won't know how much to spend on acquiring or retaining a customer if you do not know what that customer is worth to your business.

Traditional Methods are Flawed

Conventional methods of calculating CLV consider a limited time horizon and treat every customer as an average customer, blurring key metric drivers. On top of that, most CLV forecasting is based on regression analysis, which is notorously poor at predicting the future. Our customer equity platform uses fully probabilistic models at an individual customer level, and our methods come from the latest research on modern CLV calculation.

Pairs Well with Loyalty Programs

Consumers are informed and empowered with limitless buying options. The retailers that win are the ones that understand how to service relationships with their most profitable customers. In a world where most companies focused on loyalty show weaker revenue and profit (McKinsey), understanding the CLV of each idividual in your program will give you an unparalleled advantage.
